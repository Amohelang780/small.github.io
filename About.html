About Us

Curious Child was founded in 2021 with the aim of providing high-quality products and services to our customers. Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence in customer service and innovative solutions.Our factory, Curious Child is oldest wool clothes manufacturer in Lesotho and is located in Mokhotlong Popa.We have been successfully by Basotho since the business started, we are very proud to earn all Basotho trust on selling this unique CC brand to them.

Our Aims & Objectives:

  1. To provide top-notch products and services to our customers
  2. To continuously improve our offerings through innovation and research
  3. To foster a culture of excellence and teamwork within the organization
